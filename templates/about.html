About Us: Empowering Sustainable Gas Turbine Operations with Data-Driven Insights


I'm Geetanshu Dev Meshram, and I'm part of a passionate team of engineers at IIIT Naya Raipur dedicated to unlocking the potential of data-driven insights for gas turbine technology. We firmly believe that by leveraging the power of data, we can significantly optimize performance, minimize emissions, and ensure the long-term, sustainable operation of gas turbines.

Our Mission:
Here at IIIT Naya Raipur, we're driven by a clear mission:
  • Revolutionize gas turbine performance prediction: We've developed a groundbreaking online platform that empowers users to predict CO and NOx emission levels for their gas turbines. By simply entering nine key data points, our cutting-edge algorithms will generate a comprehensive prediction, providing valuable foresight into your turbine's behavior.
  • Proactive anomaly detection: Our platform goes beyond prediction. It actively identifies potential anomalies in your CO and NOx emission levels, giving you a crucial advantage. With this early warning system, you can take corrective action before minor issues escalate into significant problems, ensuring efficient and compliant operation.
  • Foster a data-driven future: We're strong advocates for the transformative power of data. Our platform offers valuable insights that can help you optimize gas turbine maintenance schedules, reduce fuel consumption, and minimize your environmental impact.
